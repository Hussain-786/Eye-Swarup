Contact Lens

The contact lens department at Swarup Eye Centre is well equipped to cater to the requirements of all eye conditions. Swarup Eye Centre provides a range of contact lenses. ABOUT US

Swarup Eye Centre is a super-specialty eye hospital in Hyderabad since 1961. Swarup Eye Centre started as a small clinic but has evolved into a premier institution for eye care in Hyderabad, offering quality services to thousands of patients every year from around the world.

Swarup Eye Centre represents three generations of commitment & dedication to the field of Ophthalmology by the Swarup family and over three generations of trust that patients and their families have placed in us.

Swarup Eye Centre has five branches in Hyderabad, located at Banjara Hills – Road No 1, Chapel Road – Abids, Attapur, Olive Hospitals and MediCiti Hospitals. Our hospital is well equipped with state of the art diagnostic & surgical equipment’s and world-class operation theaters.
